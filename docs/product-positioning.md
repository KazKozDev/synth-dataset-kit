# Product Positioning

## One-Sentence Positioning

`Synth Dataset Kit turns 20-50 real examples into an auditable fine-tuning dataset faster than framework-heavy synthetic-data stacks.`

## Who This Is For

- solo ML engineers
- applied LLM engineers
- small teams with real seed data and no patience for pipeline orchestration

## Who This Is Not For

- teams looking for a general synthetic-data research framework
- teams that want to design custom DAGs before seeing output
- academic-first experimentation without time-to-value pressure

## Product Promise

- start from your own seed examples
- generate a dataset in the same rough distribution
- score it transparently
- check contamination
- prepare proof and publication artifacts

## Why This Wins

- faster time-to-value than framework-first tools
- stronger transparency than black-box “quality control”
- local-first path via Ollama
- built-in proof and publication workflow

## What Not To Drift Into

- generic pipeline framework positioning
- “supports everything” messaging
- feature-count competition
- front-end-first storytelling

## Canonical Product Story

`I have 20-50 real examples and I need a usable, auditable fine-tuning dataset today.`
